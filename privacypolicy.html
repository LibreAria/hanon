Privacy Policy

Orchestral Excerpts does not and will not collect any information from its users whatsoever. This website respects the user; no ads, no cookies, no trackers.

Orchestral Excerpts uses localStorage for the sake of enabling dark mode for the entire site. The localStorage data only contains whether or not dark mode is preferred and does not contain any identifiers. Furthermore, this is entirely client side and does not send any information to Orchestral Excerpts.

Orchestral Excerpts is entirely free and open source under GPLv3. For more information, please visit the Orchestral Excerpts GitHub Repository.
